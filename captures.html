Captures

We take privacy seriously.

Try it out

What happens when I take a photo?

Nothing gets saved until you send the photo. You can redo a capture until you are satisfied to send it to your friends.

What happens when I'm ready to send it?

It will be saved onto our servers and will only show it to people you sent it to.

When does the photo get deleted from servers?

The original photo gets deleted after the last person on your sent list sees the photo.

On what device can I take a picture on?

As long as your device can run a browser and has a camera attached to it, you can capture. This means you can capture photos on your computer, phone, etc.
