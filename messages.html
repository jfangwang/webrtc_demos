What are messages?

Messages is a place where a user will receive snaps from other users on the platform. We are currently working on a friends list feature and hope to release soon!

Where can I view my messages?

You can view your messages on any device that can run a browser. This means you can send a photo from your phone and see it on your computer!

When does the photo get deleted from servers?

The original photo gets deleted after the last person on your sent list sees the photo.

Why are some photos look too big or too small?

We adjust the photo to the best size for your device. Sometimes we just can't adjust the photo otherwise it would look weird. This is a feature we are currently working on.
