Project Yellow Ghost

Our mission statement is to prove this concept could work and show companies they should look at webapps again for their products.

Try it out

What we are NOT trying to solve

We are NOT trying to replace SnapChat. We just want to test out this concept and see if it works well enough to possibly replace apps with webapps. We just happened to pick SnapChat as our test product since millions of people use their product everyday.

Who will be impacted by this?

This will make a positive impact on the web development community showing the capabilities of what webapps can do.

What is this built on?

React JS is the backbone of this entire project and the community is one of the biggest in the webframe category. Unless Facebook decides to shutdown React JS, we believe this technology will be able to support the capabilities of this project.
